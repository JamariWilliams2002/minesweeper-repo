1. https://github.com/github/gitignore
A collection of gitignore templates.
Usefeul for different applications, languages, games, and more. 
Quick for different when you're working on different projects and don't want to create a gitignore yourself.

2. https://github.com/Pathoschild/SMAPI
Allows for modding in Stardew Valley.
It automatically creates backups for your saves, 
lets you know when there are updates for the game,
and provides detailed information on errors. 

3. https://github.com/forrestthewoods/d3js_smash_brothers
A post about potential balancing for Super Smash Brothers Melee. 
It allows users to make any balance changes to the game that they see fit. 

4. https://github.com/KevinLage/DeadCells-Cheat
Allows for cheats in Dead Cells
One of those cheats is teleportation, which makes the game significantly easier
and for some cool out of bounds techniques. 

5. https://github.com/uberhalit/EldenRingFpsUnlockAndMore
Drops the frame rate lock on Elden Ring. 
Makes the game look smoother, allows widescreen support,
can stop you from losing runes upon death, and increase the overall speed of the game. 
Doesn't change the files of the game, only patches the RAM
